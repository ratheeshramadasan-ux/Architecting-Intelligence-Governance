Combined risk decision

AI type × data sensitivity × business impact × autonomy × external exposure × reversibility

Low

Internal assistance, approved information, no material decision and human validation.

High

Confidential data, customer-visible content, regulated advice, integration or difficult-to-reverse outcomes.

Critical

Autonomous action, financial or legal impact, restricted data, MCP execution or multi-agent delegation.

Dynamic SOP and operational knowledge governance

Change Identification

AI Impact Assessment

Identify affected agents, prompts, business rules, retrieval indexes, MCP tools and approval routes.

Controlled Activation

Re-index, re-embed, regression test, approve, release, monitor and retire obsolete guidance.

No confirmed and applicable SOP means no autonomous execution.

Evidence, supply chain and retirement

Decision Reconstruction

User or event, identity, model, prompt, SOP, sources, retrieval, tools, approvals and final action.

AI Supply Chain

Model and dataset provenance, licences, subprocessors, open-source integrity and MCP/tool registry.

Retirement

Disable agents, revoke identities and tools, remove embeddings, retain evidence and verify exit.
